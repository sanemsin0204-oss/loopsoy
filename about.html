About Loopsoy

A soft corner of the internet for making beautiful things, one loop at a time.

Why I started

I have always loved making things I can actually use.

Before crochet, I tried a little bit of everything: macramé, resin art, arts and crafts, and paper craft. Crochet was the one that finally stuck.

It has been about thirteen months since I picked up my first hook, and now I have a cupboard full of crochet supplies and projects—mostly completed. 😊

My philosophy

Beautiful handmade creations deserve beautiful materials. That does not always mean the most expensive supplies; it means choosing yarn, tools, and finishing details that make the process feel good from the first stitch to the last end woven in.

What you will find here

You will find the actual projects I created, the supplies I used, honest pro tips, and the YouTube tutorials that helped me make them. Some supply links are Amazon affiliate links—if you find Loopsoy useful, purchasing through them is a small, lovely way to show support at no extra cost to you.
